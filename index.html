About Me

My name is Mehul Suresh, I'm 22 years old and I just graduated with my Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of South Florida in Tampa, Florida. What do I do? I code. I started my programming journey many years ago with a TI-82 calculator, and since that day I knew my true calling.
